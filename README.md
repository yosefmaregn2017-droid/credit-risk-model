# Credit Risk Model Project

## 1. Credit Scoring Business Understanding
This section explains the business problem and why credit scoring is important.

Credit scoring is used by financial institutions to:
- Evaluate the likelihood that a borrower will default.
- Segment customers by risk.
- Optimize lending decisions.
- Reduce financial losses.
- Improve approval and decline strategies.

The goal of this project is to identify the key drivers of credit risk and build a foundation for developing a predictive model.
